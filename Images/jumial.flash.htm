Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does the Flash Sale work?

During Jumia's Flash Sale, selected products are offered to the public at highly subsidized prices. However, please note that the number of products available is limited. These flash sales go live at a specified time, and due to their popularity and limited quantity, they sell out very quickly, often within seconds. Keep in mind that there are tens of thousands of people trying to buy the same product, so it's a case of the fastest fingers first.

Can I purchase more than one item during a Flash Sale?

No, Flash Sales on Jumia are limited to one item per customer. This ensures that more customers have the opportunity to benefit from the fantastic deals available.

What should I consider when participating in a Flash Sale?

To have the best chance of securing a flash sale item, you need to be quick with your device and have a fast and stable internet connection. Keep in mind that finding an item and adding it to your cart does not guarantee that you will be able to check out before others. It's important to act swiftly and be prepared for the competitive nature of flash sales.

Can Jumia cancel my order for a flash sale item?

Yes, Jumia reserves the right to cancel orders that it deems fraudulent. This measure ensures a fair shopping experience for all customers and maintains the integrity of the flash sale event.

Are there any restrictions on who can participate in Flash Sales?

Flash sales on Jumia are open to the public; however, all staff, family of staff, partners, and agents are not eligible to participate.

Can Jumia change the products offered for Flash Sales?

Yes, Jumia reserves the right to change the products offered for flash sales. This allows for variety and ensures that customers can discover new and exciting deals with each flash sale event.
