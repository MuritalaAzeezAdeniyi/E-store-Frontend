Product details

New LONG LIFE TYPE Rechargeable 16 inches standing fan from the QLINK / QASA Company. DURAVOLT is a sister brand produced by the same company as the household name brand -QASA.


1. Lithium Ion battery type - Li-ion batteries are known for their ruggedness and durability and can last for about 3 or 4 years, with over a thousand recharge cycles. Thats why the product series is nicknamed the "the LONG LIFE RECHARGEABLE FAN. With this product, you wave bye to changing batteries every 9 months to 1 year

2. Dual-way charging. This unique fan can be charged in 2 ways viz

  • With Alternating current (NEPA/GEN power)
  •  Solar Charging (SOLAR PANEL  INCLUDED)- The product can be charged with a 10 or 20 watts watts solar panel to provide an alternative means of charging, making the fan suitable for use in areas with epileptic power supply or No power supply at all. This fan can be used in rural areas without any hassle

3. Low Power Consumption - Our unique fan consumes very little power as it is rated at 16 watts. This ensures you have a longer fanning time and when charging or using with NEPA,light, you worry less about your monthly PREPAID bills


4. 5 variable speeds - Our product comes with 5 different speeds with the lowest speed lasting for up to 25 hours , at full charge

Speed 1 : Approx 4 hours

Speed 2 : Approx 8 hours

Speed 3 : Approx 13 hours

Speed 4: Approx 17 hours

Speed 5: Approx 24 to 25 hours

(Note = The above is estimated based on a full charge)

5. Remote controlled Rotation: I bet you must have experienced having to stand up, sometimes in the middle of the night, to adjust the rotation control, either to make the fan rotate or stop the fan from rotating. That is now a thing of the past as you can do that using the remote control that accompanies the fan. a timer button is also included
